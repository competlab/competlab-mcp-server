# Example Prompts

Once CompetLab MCP is connected, try these prompts with your AI agent. All prompts work with Claude, Cursor, VS Code Copilot, and other MCP clients.

## Getting Started

> "List my CompetLab projects"

> "Show me the project details and when each dimension was last monitored"

> "Who are the competitors I'm tracking?"

## AI Visibility (unique to CompetLab)

> "How does ChatGPT rank my brand vs competitors?"

> "What's my AI Visibility Score and how does it compare to competitors?"

> "Show me how my AI visibility has changed over the last 3 months"

> "Filter the AI visibility trend for just OpenAI — are we gaining or losing ground?"

> "Which competitor has the highest mention rate across all LLMs?"

## Competitive Action Plan

> "Show me the action plan — what should I fix first?"

> "What are the top competitive threats I should address this week?"

> "Give me the full action plan with evidence and affected competitors"

## Pricing Intelligence

> "What changed on my competitors' pricing pages recently?"

> "Compare pricing plans across all competitors — who offers what at each tier?"

> "Show me critical pricing alerts from the last 7 days"

> "Pull the pricing gap analysis — where are we over/under-priced?"

## Content Intelligence

> "Compare content strategies across all tracked competitors"

> "What new URLs did competitors add to their sitemaps this week?"

> "Show me content changes for competitor X — filter by blog posts only"

> "Which competitor has the most docs/landing pages?"

## Positioning

> "How do competitors position themselves on their homepages?"

> "Compare our value proposition vs the top 3 competitors"

> "What CTAs are competitors using? Any patterns?"

## Tech & Trust

> "Which competitors have better security headers than us?"

> "Compare trust signals — who has the most compliance badges?"

> "Are any competitors blocking AI crawlers in their robots.txt?"

> "What tech stack changes have competitors made recently?"

## Alerts & Monitoring

> "What alerts fired in the last 7 days?"

> "Show me all critical alerts across all dimensions"

> "What's our monitoring schedule? When is the next run?"

> "Filter alerts for just pricing changes from competitor X"

## Reports & Analysis

> "Build me a competitive briefing I can share with the team"

> "Summarize all 5 dimensions for our main competitor"

> "Create a weekly competitive digest with the most important changes"

> "What are the biggest gaps between us and the market leader?"
